SEVEN WONDERS OF THE WORLD

  1. Great Wall of China

    • Huairou District, China
    • Fun Fact: It's the largest man-made project in the world



  2. Christ the Redeemer

    • Corcovado Mountain, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
    • Fun Fact: It's 98 feet tall



  3. Machu Picchu

    • Andes Mountains, Peru
    • Fun Fact: It was constructed without wheels being used



  4. Petra

    • Ma'an Governorate of Jordan
    • Fun Fact: It's sometimes called the Rose-Red City



  5. Chichen Itza

    • Yucatan, Mexico
    • Fun Fact: The city covers around four square kilometers



  6. Taj Mahal

    • Agra, Uttar Pradest, India
    • Fun Fact: It was built with 27 different types of stones



  7. Colosseum

    • Rome, Italy
    • Fun Fact: Originally named the Flavian Amphitheater
